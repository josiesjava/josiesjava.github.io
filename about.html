Our Story

Josie’s Java was inspired by a love of coffee, community, and compassion — and named after our sweet rescue pup, Josie, who reminds us every day to lead with kindness and wag more, bark less.

We’re a locally owned drive-thru coffee trailer proudly serving Richmond and Rosenberg — bringing great coffee, friendly service, and community spirit wherever we go.

Our Values

  • Animal-Loving: A portion of profits is donated to local rescues and nonprofits each quarter.
  • Eco-Conscious: We aim to reduce waste by using compostable cups, recyclable materials, and sustainably sourced ingredients whenever possible.
  • Community-Driven: You’ll find us at local markets and events, supporting causes that matter and neighbors we care about.

Whether you’re grabbing your morning brew, treating yourself to a smoothie, or picking up pup cups for the pack — we’re here for it.

Thanks for supporting a local business with a big heart.
