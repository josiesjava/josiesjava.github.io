About Josie’s Java

Josie’s Java is a double-sided drive-thru coffee trailer in Richmond/Rosenberg, TX.

We serve handcrafted coffee, smoothies, tea, and baked goods with a smile — and donate a portion of our profits to animal rescues and local causes each quarter.

Founded by two animal-loving caffeine addicts with a dream of building something warm and community-driven.
